Sports
Facilities

At our College Campus, we aim for perfection in competitive sport as in all else. Team Sports are played on an inter-college. The faculty includes professional coaches for all sports and we set out to defeat the opposition with skill and good humor.

Sports facilities include Badminton court with all the required instruments. College has faculties for training students. Players or participants will be encouraged to be on time, to dress appropriately and tidily, to great opponents in a friendly manner before a competition and thank both them and the umpire/ referee afterwards, to play in a team spirit to the best of their ability, to encourage team mates, and to win and lose in civilize manner.More over College is also providing facilities to play Chess, Carom, and Badminton etc. Some of our students achievements are:

  • Sajal Srivastava from EC Branch was a State Level Champion in Badminton in the year 2006 and also participated in the National Tournament.
  • Meeta Dubey from EC Branch was a State Level Champion in Badminton in the year 2005.
  • G.G.I.T.S Football team was a Runner up in the year 2005 in the Nodal Center Level Championship.
  • Manoj Sujan from ME Branch was a State Level Runner up - Table Tennis in the year 2007.
  • Reetesh Jain from ME Branch participated in the National Level Hand Ball Championship in the year 2008.
  • A national level Programme ‘TECHKRITI’ was organized at I.I.T, Kanpur from 14 to 17 Feb, 2008. Aniket Diwivedi and Ketan Kothari from EC branch (Batch 2006-10) won second prize.
  • I I ITDM organized a grand function on 8, 9, 10 Feb. 2008 in Jabalpur Engineering College and a number of students from GGITS participated in various events like Debate, Speed sketching, Group Dance, Solo Dance, etc. and secured good positions.
  • Blood Donation Camps were organized by GGITS on 10,11 Mar. ’07, 15 Mar. ’08 and 9 Aug. ’08 for the welfare of the society.
